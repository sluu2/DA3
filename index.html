About

High Concept: The game is basically Mario as a puzzle platformer.

Player Experience Goals: The goal of this game is to immerse the player as he or she is traversing the procedurally generated world

Genre: The genre of this game will be a 2D puzzle platformer.

Game play: Currently the only new action that the player can perform is a walljump and wallslide. In future patches, there'll be more dynamic actions that the player can excute.

Prototype Goal: The goal of this iteration is to evaluate the smoothness of movement.

Setting/Premise: No story is apparent at the moment, since the tilemap setting is that of a testing room (similar to Valve's Portal). In later iterations, a new tilemap will be used.

Story: I guess that Mario has to save Princess Peach again?

Target Audience: The target audience that this game would appeal to are people of all ages.

Play time: In the current alpha, the game will take less than 5 min to complete.

Strategies: Don't fall, that's it!

Prototype Closest Other Game: Using the game Mario as the inspiration for the concept of the game and the Phaser Tutorial as the template to start off on
